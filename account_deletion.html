How to Delete Your Account

⚠️ Important: Deleting your account will permanently remove all your data, including booking history, profile information, and social posts. This action cannot be undone.

Option 1: Delete Account In-App (Recommended)

The easiest way to delete your account is directly through the VRCSC app:

  1. Open the VRCSC app on your device
  2. Go to your Profile or Settings
  3. Look for "Account Settings" or "Delete Account" option
  4. Follow the prompts to permanently delete your account
  5. Confirm the deletion when prompted

Your account and all associated data will be deleted immediately.

Option 2: Contact Support

If you're unable to delete your account through the app, you can contact our support team:

  • Email: support@vrcsportscenter.com
  • Subject: Account Deletion Request
  • Include: Your registered email address and reason for deletion

We will process your request within 7 business days and send you a confirmation email once your account has been deleted.

What Data Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Profile information (name, email, phone number, profile picture)
  • Booking history and preferences
  • Social media posts and interactions
  • Payment information (if any)
  • App settings and preferences
  • All personal data associated with your account
